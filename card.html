Bottlenose Dolphin

Dolphins are carnivores.

  • Scientific Name: Tursiops
  • Average Length: 2.5 meters
  • Average Lifespan: 42.5
  • Habitat: Ocean

Bottlenose dolphins give the appearance that they are always smiling with their short thick beak and curved mouth. They are marine mammals that live in oceans around the world with mild temperatures. They can reach an average length of 2.5 meters.
